## Rotation rule

Never apply two consecutive sprays from the same FRAC group. The vigilance agent should track recent applications via Memory and refuse to recommend the same group twice in a row.

## Application discipline

- Spray at dawn (≤ 06h30) or dusk (≥ 17h30) — never midday.
- Cover the underside of leaves; that's where sporulation lives.
- Wait until dew has lifted before spraying contact products.
- Re-spray after rain > 8 mm within 4 hours of application.

## Organic-only farms

Skip mancozeb / metalaxyl / cymoxanil / azoxystrobin entirely. Use Bordeaux mixture or copper hydroxide labelled for organic production, plus aggressive sanitation (remove infected leaves twice weekly), wider plant spacing, drip irrigation timed for morning to dry foliage by night.
